### 1:Bike Sales Analysis Project (Pivot Tables & Visualizations)
- The dataset contains information about who is buying bikes, where they live, and how much profit the company is making. It is a great resource for learning how to use Excel Pivot Tables and visualizations to find sales trends.
  
### What’s in the data?
- Customer Info: Age, gender, and location (Country and State).

- Product Info: What kind of bike or accessory was sold (Category and Description).

- The Numbers: How many were sold, how much they cost, and the final Revenue and Profit.
  
### Why this project is useful:
I used this data to answer simple business questions like:

Which country buys the most bikes?

Do men or women spend more money?

Which age group is the most profitable for the business?
